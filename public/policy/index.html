Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2024

Welcome to PDF2Image. We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our service.

1. Information Collection

PDF2Image is designed with privacy as a priority:

  • PDF Files: All files you upload are processed directly in your browser. No files are sent to our servers.
  • Technical Data: We may collect anonymous information about your device and browser to improve our service.
  • Cookies: We only use essential cookies for site functionality. No tracking cookies are used.

2. Use of Information

The data we collect is used exclusively for:

  • Providing and improving our conversion service
  • Analyzing site usage in anonymous, aggregated form
  • Detecting and preventing technical issues

3. Data Protection

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

  • Local Processing: All conversions happen directly in your browser
  • No Storage: Files are automatically deleted after conversion
  • Secure Connection: HTTPS encryption for all communications

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For anonymous audience analysis
  • Google Tag Manager: For managing analytics tags

These services may collect anonymous data according to their own privacy policies. You can disable Google Analytics through your browser settings.

  • To data portability
